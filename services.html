Delivery

Morris Iron & Steel accepts all grades of steel and stainless steel commodities.
For individuals interested in coming to our facility to scrap smaller loads of material, we offer a location in Hatfield, Pa. Which will allow you to scrap both ferrous and non-ferrous material. Contact this location for up to the minute pricing call (215)-855-7690 or email us at: scale@emertrecycling.com
  • All material must be driven into the scrap yard by a personal vehicle.
  • Sellers must be prepared to show a driver’s license.

Pickup

Morris Iron & Steel has a team of full-time in house drivers, along with a trusted network of contract haulers to provide top notch service to all of our customers. Our fleet is fully equipped to provide the following services for manufactures, job sites, service centers and other business:
  • Roll Off Dumpster Rentals (30 & 40 yard)
  • Lugger Bin Rentals
  • Flatbed Trailers
  • Van Trailers
  • Steel & Aluminum Dump Trailers
We offer full-service for industrial and demolition accounts within 100 miles of Philadelphia, PA.Please email us at dispatch@morrisironandsteel.com or visit the contact us page for information on setting up this service and pricing.
