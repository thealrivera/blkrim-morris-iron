Morris Iron & Steel is a family owned, fourth generation company that has operated since 1938. We take pride in serving the scrap metal and recycling needs of the eastern part of Pennsylvania for more than 75 years.

  • We are able to transport our material by barge, containers and rail through CSX and NS lines and trucks
  • Morris Iron supplies major domestic steel mills throughout the country
  • In addition to our major yard operations we also have a large brokerage division

WHAT'S NEW

Pier Facility

Read Press Release
Morris Iron completed restoring 1,067 linear feet of the pier at our 7777 State Road location, so that the existing structure can continue to be operational. Morris Iron was granted funding through RACP grant. This will allow Morris Iron to service 3 barges simultaneously. Shipment by barge significantly reduces truck and rail traffic, and loading barges is inherently safer than loading trucks or railcars. This project will also enhance the safety and stability of the roadways and loading areas of the facility that are used by commercial trucks and equipment. In addition, this project will increase Morris Iron’s capacity to process and recycle more scrap metal, lower carbon emissions, improve public safety, and create more jobs for the Philadelphia area.
